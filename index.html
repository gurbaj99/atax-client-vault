🏥 Health Insurance Documents

📌 Don't have your documents? Get them from IRS.gov:

  1. Go to IRS.gov → Click "Create New Account" with ID.me
  2. Select "Individual" account (NOT Business)
  3. Choose "Text Message" for authentication
  4. Choose "Self Service" for verification (NOT video call)
  5. For W-2, 1099, 1095-A: Go to Account Home → Tax Records → View/Download
  6. For Last Year Tax Return: Go to Account Home → Tax Records → Download Tax Transcript
